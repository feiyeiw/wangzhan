About 1³ Machine

Your trusted partner for automated production and smart warehouse solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2022, 1³ Machine has quickly established itself as a leading provider of automated production and smart warehouse solutions. Based in Hong Kong, we serve clients worldwide with our expertise in system integration and project delivery.

Our mission is to help manufacturers optimize their operations through advanced automation technology, reducing costs, improving efficiency, and ensuring consistent product quality.

Our Approach

As a client-side project partner and system integrator, we take a holistic approach to every project. We work closely with our clients to understand their specific needs and challenges, then design customized solutions that address their unique requirements.

Our team of experienced engineers and project managers ensures that every project is delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.
